Hi! I'm Chris. I'm a full-stack web developer currently based in Wellingon, New Zealand.


After three years working as a civil engineer at a consultancy I have developed the skills to develop practical solutions to complex problems in a professional environment, while meeting the needs of the client and of the company. Over this period I began to realise the aspects of my career I excelled at and got the most satisfaction from were those most closely related to the software side rather than the practical side of engineering. Having had a taste of coding through my degree, I knew it was something I was interested in and had a strong aptitude for, and after a few months of learning to code on my own that interest turned into a passion. Now I aim to turn that passion into a career.
