Hi! I'm Chris. I'm a full-stack web developer currently based in Wellington, New Zealand.

I have recently completed an intense three month full-stack software engineering course with General Assembly. I come from an engineering background, and after three years working as a Civil Engineer I have developed the skills to produce practical solutions to complex problems in a professional environment, while meeting the needs of the client and of the company. Over this period I began to realise the aspects of my career I excelled at, and got the most satisfaction from, were those most closely related to the software side rather than the practical side of engineering. Having had a taste of coding through my degree, I knew it was something I was interested in and had a strong aptitude for, and after a few months of learning to code on my own that interest turned into a passion, and I enrolled in the three month coding bootcamp. Now with my new skills from General Assembly, I am turning that passion into a career.
